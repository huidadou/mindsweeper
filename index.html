Agentic maths assessment · v0.3

Every learner has
hidden mines.

Most assessments find the wrong one — they catch what a student got wrong, not why. Mindsweeper sweeps the mathematical mind: through Socratic conversation and past-work analysis, it charts a learner's knowledge across every strand and reveals their mathematical character — how they reason, what drives them, how they face difficulty. What emerges is not a score. It's a map.

Mindsweeper doesn't test. It probes — the way a great teacher would if they had an hour alone with one student and the patience to truly look.
